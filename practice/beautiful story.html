Prettiest Girl Haswika

Dear Niece

        The First time I saw you,
        I felt such love inside
        For a Pretty little baby girl
        who hardly ever cried.
        Soon,You were grow up a girl,
        And I was Proud of you.
        You Always did Your Best In School
        And in everything You do.
        Teenage days-they Come and Go.
        You Survived them well.
        You had your dreams and set your goals.
        I Knew you wouldn't fail.
        My Precious Niece - a gift from My lovely brother;
        You've grown up so very fast;
        But the specialness inside of you
        I know will always last....
